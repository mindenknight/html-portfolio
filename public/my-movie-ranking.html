The Best Movies According to Justin

My top 3 movies of all-time

Sam Raimi's Spider-Man

This is one of the best movies ever and kicks off the trilogy with Tobey Maguire.
I watched this movie non-stop as a kid.

Sam Raimi's Spider-Man 2

It's a sequel that is absolutely amazing! Doc Ock never looked better than in this movie.
Also, the epic Spider-Man 2 video game exists because of this movie!

Sam Raimi's Spider-Man 3

Continues the amazing story of the first 2 while throwing everything but the kitchen sink at Spider-Man.
Bonus points as the most memeable of the trilogy and because Bully Maguire
